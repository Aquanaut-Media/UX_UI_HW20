Hello!

I'm Grant Ertl

Graphic designer and illustrator with a background in video production and editing. Attended Minnesota State University Moorhead for Video Production. Recently received a certificate in UX/UI from the University of Minnesota, Minneapolis with skills in UX Research, HTML5, CSS, Javascript, Visual Prototyping, Wireframing, and User Interface Design. Forward-thinking professional eager to solve real-world problems through research, collaboration, and creativity. Excellent eye for design, strong communication skills, capable of taking projects from start to finish with the dedication to make sure they’re completed on time. Capable of any creative task required from wireframe sketching to interaction design. Conducted interviews with international travelers for a recent product design project where I created an app to help travelers keep track of their plans and store travel documents offline. Iterated on lo-fi prototypes to create a fleshed out and streamlined app with a sharp modern user interface. Excellent visual design skills, interpersonal ability, and adaptability make me an excellent UX designer candidate.
